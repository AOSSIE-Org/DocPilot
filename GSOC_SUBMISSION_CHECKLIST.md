## 💡 Pro Tips

### Stand Out from Other Applicants
1. **Show, don't just tell** - Your 15,000 LOC contribution speaks volumes
2. **Be specific** - "Add appointment system" vs "Build OPD booking with 5 screens"
3. **Demonstrate healthcare knowledge** - Use medical terms correctly (SOAP, ESI, I-PASS)
4. **Commit to long-term** - Mention post-GSoC maintenance plans
5. **Be realistic** - Don't overpromise; your timeline should be achievable

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Submitting too many proposals (stick to 1-2)
- ❌ Generic proposals that could apply to any project
- ❌ Unrealistic timelines (building entire EHR in 1 week)
- ❌ Ignoring mentor feedback
- ❌ Submitting at the last minute (missed deadline = auto-reject)
- ❌ Not proofreading (typos signal carelessness)
- ❌ Copying proposals from previous years

### What Mentors Look For
1. **Technical competence** - Can you actually build this?
2. **Communication skills** - Will you be easy to work with?
3. **Genuine interest** - Do you care about healthcare impact?
4. **Reliability** - Will you complete the project?
5. **Community fit** - Do you align with AOSSIE values?
